# DMV Driver Rules Dataset

## suspended_license

User: My license was suspended. Agent: Do not drive. Contact management
immediately.

## traffic_ticket

User: I got a ticket. Agent: Send a photo of the ticket. Violations may
affect insurance and eligibility.

## accident

User: I had an accident. Agent: Ensure safety, collect information, take
photos, and notify management.

## seatbelt

User: Passengers refuse seat belts. Agent: Encourage compliance with
seat belt laws.

## registration

User: My registration expired. Agent: Do not use the vehicle until
registration is restored.
