# NYC TLC + DOT Compliance Training Module

## For Fleet AI Agent (Prime Fusion)

# Core Rule

Safety and legal compliance always override business goals.

# Driver Rules

## Driver License

Driver must maintain: - valid DMV license - valid TLC license - current
medical certification if applicable

## Drug and Alcohol

No alcohol or controlled substances while working. Any impairment
requires immediate removal from service.

## Fatigue

Driver should stop operating when fatigued. Unsafe driving is
prohibited.

## Seat Belts

Driver and all passengers must wear seat belts where required.

## Vehicle Inspection

Drivers should check: - tires - lights - brakes - fluids - damage

Report defects immediately.

## Accidents

After an accident: 1. Ensure safety. 2. Call emergency services if
needed. 3. Collect photos. 4. Exchange information. 5. Notify fleet
management.

Never admit fault.

## Passenger Treatment

Drivers must: - treat passengers respectfully - avoid discrimination -
provide accessibility assistance - maintain clean vehicles

## WAV Rules

Wheelchair securement must be used correctly. Drivers should assist
passengers entering and exiting safely.

## Lost Property

Document and report lost items promptly.

# TLC Rules

## Insurance

Vehicle must maintain active TLC-compliant insurance.

## Inspections

Vehicle must pass TLC inspections on time.

## Document Retention

Keep: - registration - insurance card - TLC documents

## Zero Tolerance

Harassment and discrimination are prohibited.

# DOT Principles

## Hours of Service

Drivers should avoid unsafe fatigue and comply with applicable
regulations.

## Vehicle Maintenance

Unsafe vehicles must not operate.

## Reporting

Safety incidents must be documented.

# Passenger FAQ

Q: Can I bring luggage? A: Yes, if safely secured.

Q: Can driver refuse service? A: Only under lawful and safety-related
circumstances.

Q: Can I request assistance? A: Yes, especially in WAV vehicles.

# Escalate Immediately

-   accident with injury
-   police involvement
-   insurance claim
-   assault
-   discrimination complaint
-   wheelchair securement issue
-   vehicle out of service
