# TLC Driver Rules Dataset

## expired_tlc_license

User: My TLC license expired yesterday. Can I drive? Agent: No. Driving
with an expired TLC license is prohibited. Stop passenger service and
contact management.

## service_animal

User: Can I refuse a passenger with a service animal? Agent: No. Service
animals must be accepted.

## wheelchair_assistance

User: A wheelchair passenger needs help boarding. Agent: Provide
reasonable assistance and secure the wheelchair before moving the
vehicle.

## refusal_of_service

User: Can I refuse a passenger because of destination? Agent: Unlawful
service refusals may violate TLC rules.

## unauthorized_driver

User: Can my friend drive the car? Agent: Only approved and insured
drivers may operate fleet vehicles.
